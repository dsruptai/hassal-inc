About Hassal Inc

Hassal Inc is an intelligence platform that monitors and aggregates South African business sales, acquisitions, mergers, and liquidity events. We continuously scan multiple trusted sources to surface deal activity in the South African market.

What We Track

  • Acquisitions & Mergers — Company buyouts, mergers, and schemes of arrangement
  • Delistings — Companies leaving the JSE
  • Stake Sales — Significant share purchases and disposals
  • Private Equity — PE-backed transactions and buyouts
  • BEE Transactions — B-BBEE ownership deals
  • Business Rescue — Companies entering or exiting business rescue
  • Competition Commission Rulings — Merger approvals and conditions
  • Cautionary Announcements — Firm intentions and offers on the JSE
  • Joint Ventures & Recapitalisations

How It Works

Hassal Inc automatically scans all sources daily. Each article and announcement is analysed against a comprehensive set of M&A and liquidity-event keywords, scored by relevance, and categorised by deal type.

API

  • GET /api/deals — List deals (?search=, ?source=, ?deal_type=, ?limit=, ?offset=)
  • GET /api/sources — Source statistics
  • GET /api/deal-types — Deal type breakdown
  • POST /api/scrape — Trigger manual refresh
