What is Hassal Inc?

Hassal Inc is an intelligence platform that monitors and aggregates South African business sales, acquisitions, mergers, and liquidity events. We continuously scan multiple trusted sources to bring you a comprehensive view of deal activity in the South African market.

What We Track

  • Acquisitions & Mergers — Company buyouts, mergers, and schemes of arrangement
  • Delistings — Companies leaving the JSE
  • Stake Sales — Significant share purchases and disposals
  • Private Equity — PE-backed transactions and buyouts
  • BEE Transactions — B-BBEE ownership deals
  • Business Rescue — Companies entering or exiting business rescue
  • Competition Commission Rulings — Merger approvals and conditions
  • Cautionary Announcements — Firm intentions and offers on the JSE
  • Joint Ventures — New JV formations and changes
  • Recapitalisations — Capital restructuring events

Our Sources

We aggregate data from the following South African business and financial news sources:

JSE SENS (Stock Exchange News Service)
Official mandatory disclosure channel for all JSE-listed companies. Includes cautionary announcements, firm intentions, and scheme details. Competition Commission South Africa
Official regulator for merger approvals. All large and intermediate mergers must be approved by the Competition Commission before they can proceed. Tracks approval decisions, conditions, and prohibited mergers. DealMakers South Africa
Tracks and records M&A and General Corporate Finance activity for both listed and unlisted SA companies. Published quarterly since 2000. Moneyweb
Leading South African financial news site covering markets, companies, and deals. BizNews
South African business and investment news platform. Fin24 / News24
Fin24 company news — South Africa's largest news website's financial arm. BusinessLIVE
Premium South African business journalism from Business Day and Financial Mail. IOL Business Report
Business and financial reporting from Independent Online. Engineering News

How It Works

Hassal Inc automatically scans all sources every hour. Each article and announcement is analysed against a comprehensive set of M&A and liquidity-event keywords. Articles are scored by relevance and categorised by deal type. Only content that meets a minimum relevance threshold is included in the dashboard.

API Access

Hassal Inc exposes a simple REST API for programmatic access:

  • GET /api/deals — List deals (supports ?search=, ?source=, ?deal_type=, ?limit=, ?offset=)
  • GET /api/sources — Source statistics
  • GET /api/deal-types — Deal type breakdown
  • POST /api/scrape — Trigger a manual data refresh
